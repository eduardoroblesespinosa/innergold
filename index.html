Why Am I Poor?

"Poverty is not a lack of money, it is a lack of BEING."

Law of the Level of Being

Your external life is a direct reflection of your internal state. To change your circumstances, you must first raise your level of being. You cannot achieve a higher state with a lower consciousness.

Law of Psychological Affinities

We are drawn to people, places, and situations that resonate with our inner psychological state. A mind filled with negativity and vice will naturally find itself in impoverished and desperate situations.

Like Attracts Like

Mental poverty creates material poverty. A mind obsessed with trivialities, arguments, and lust cannot attract wealth or opportunity. Spiritual poverty is sooner or later reflected in your bank account.

Psychological Death

To ascend, part of you must die. This is the technique for eliminating lustful thoughts.

1. Observe

Witness the thought without judgment. Do not identify with it. See it as an external intruder.

2. Identify

Recognize the thought for what it is: a manifestation of the lustful "I" or defect.

3. Invoke

Mentally or verbally, call upon your inner Divine Mother to obliterate the defect with her purifying fire.

4. Neutralize

Feel the desire neutralize. The energy that fed the thought is now liberated and returned to your consciousness.

Conservation of Seminal Energy

"Sperm is your inner gold. Don't spill it, transmute it."

The Creative Power

From a biological perspective, seminal fluid contains the blueprint for life. Esoterically, it is the physical condensation of creative energy, or "Christic Energy." Conserving this energy provides the raw material for spiritual work.

Transmutation Techniques

Instead of expelling this energy, we can transmute it, causing it to rise up the spinal column and awaken higher centers of consciousness. This is the secret of all true esoteric schools.

  • Pranayama: Controlled breathing exercises to direct the flow of energy (prana).
  • Mantras: Sacred words of power that cause energy to vibrate at a higher frequency, facilitating its upward movement.
  • Meditation: Focused awareness to control the mind and prevent the involuntary loss of energy through nocturnal emissions or lustful thoughts.
